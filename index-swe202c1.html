Agile va Waterfall

1. Agile la gi?

Agile la mo hinh phat trien phan mem theo kieu lap va tang dan. Tuc la khong doi lam xong toan bo he thong moi giao, ma chia du an thanh nhieu phan nho, lam tung dot, moi dot deu co san pham de review va lay feedback.

Dac diem chinh cua Agile

  • Phat trien theo iteration / sprint.
  • Lam tung chuc nang nho truoc.
  • Stakeholder feedback thuong xuyen.
  • De thay doi yeu cau.
  • Test, sua va cai tien lien tuc.

Agile hoat dong nhu the nao?

Vi du voi Course Registration System (CRS):

  • Sprint 1: login + xem course catalogue.
  • Sprint 2: check prerequisite + register course.
  • Sprint 3: waitlist + notification.
  • Sprint 4: grade management + reports.

Sau moi sprint, team demo cho stakeholder, nhan gop y, sua requirement neu can va tiep tuc sprint sau.

Uu diem cua Agile

  1. Linh hoat voi thay doi, vi du khi truong doi policy dang ky mon.
  2. Co feedback thuong xuyen tu admin, lecturer va student.
  3. Giam rui ro vi performance, security va usability duoc phat hien som.
  4. Giao hang som, khong can cho toan bo he thong hoan thanh.

Nhuoc diem cua Agile

  1. Kho quan ly neu team thieu kinh nghiem.
  2. Yeu cau stakeholder tham gia thuong xuyen.
  3. Kho du doan toan bo chi phi va thoi gian tu dau.
  4. Tai lieu co the khong chi tiet bang Waterfall neu team bo qua documentation.

2. Waterfall la gi?

Waterfall la mo hinh phat trien tuan tu theo tung giai doan. Moi giai doan phai lam xong roi moi chuyen sang giai doan tiep theo.

  1. Requirements
  2. Analysis
  3. Design
  4. Implementation
  5. Testing
  6. Deployment / Maintenance

Dac diem chinh cua Waterfall

  • Di theo trinh tu co dinh.
  • Yeu cau can ro tu dau.
  • Moi phase co tai lieu ro rang.
  • It quay lai sua phase truoc.

Uu diem cua Waterfall

  1. De quan ly vi moi giai doan ro rang.
  2. Tai lieu day du, hop voi moi truong formal nhu truong dai hoc.
  3. De uoc luong thoi gian va chi phi hon.
  4. Phu hop khi requirement on dinh.

Nhuoc diem cua Waterfall

  1. Kho thich ung khi requirement thay doi.
  2. Feedback den muon vi user chi thay he thong khi gan xong.
  3. Rui ro phat hien loi muon o giai doan testing.
  4. Khong phu hop voi du an can iterative delivery.

3. So sanh Agile va Waterfall

Tieu chi Agile Waterfall
Requirements Chap nhan requirement thay doi. Can requirement ro va on dinh ngay tu dau.
Feedback Co feedback lien tuc. Feedback thuong den muon.
Rui ro Phat hien som qua tung iteration. De phat hien muon o cuoi du an.
Quan ly Linh hoat nhung kho quan ly hon. De quan ly, quy trinh ro rang hon.
Tai lieu Co the it formal hon. Documentation manh hon.

4. Model nao hop hon cho CRS?

Voi Course Registration System, Agile thuong phu hop hon vi he thong co nhieu nhom nguoi dung nhu student, lecturer, academic staff va system admin. He thong cung co nhieu chuc nang tuong tac phuc tap nhu register course, waitlist, grade release va reports.

Agile giup xu ly tot hon cac rui ro ve concurrent-user load, security, availability va cac thay doi yeu cau thuc te tu truong trong qua trinh phat trien.

Agile is more appropriate for the CRS because it supports iterative delivery, continuous stakeholder feedback, and early handling of technical risks such as performance and security.

5. Cau viet mau ngan

Agile: Agile is an iterative and incremental software development model in which the system is developed in small releases. It emphasizes continuous stakeholder feedback, adaptability to change, and frequent testing throughout the project.

Waterfall: Waterfall is a linear and sequential software development model where each phase must be completed before moving to the next. It works best when requirements are clear, stable, and unlikely to change.

Comparison: Agile is more flexible and better suited to evolving requirements, while Waterfall provides stronger structure and documentation but is less adaptable to change.

6. Meo nho nhanh

  • Agile = chia nho, lam dan, sua linh hoat.
  • Waterfall = lam tuan tu, ro buoc, kho doi.

Ket luan: Therefore, Agile is more appropriate for the CRS project as it allows iterative development, continuous feedback, and better handling of risks such as performance, security, and changing requirements, while Waterfall is less suitable due to its rigid structure.
